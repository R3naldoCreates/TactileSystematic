Algorithm

The Tactile Systematic algorithm evaluates films across 6 weighted core dimensions, then applies 2 modifiers:

Raw = Σ(dim × weight)
Adjusted = Raw − min(Meta, 5)
Final = Adjusted × max(1−NC/100, 0.5)

Weights: Tactility 25%, Logic 20%, Competence 15%, Dialogue 15%, Momentum 15%, Grit 10%
